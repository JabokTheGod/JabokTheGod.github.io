Here are some thoughts I have about college

I would say the best parts of college are the friends that I have met and the freedom of not living at home. Besides that college brings me a lot of stress. When Covid happened I really struggled with keeping up and my motivation has swindled lower year by year.

In order for me to find college enjoyable there would have to be some changes within the system. I think that gen eds should be reduced and allow the students who know what degree they want to fast track through school. I also think that there needs to be an investment to have more academic advisors that can spend more time with students and figure things out early in school.
